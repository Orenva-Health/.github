# Orenva Health

**The connective layer for healthcare.**

Your doctor, pharmacy, lab, therapist and insurer each hold a piece of your
health — and none of them talk to each other. Orenva brings them onto one
health context, so every service reads from and writes to the same record.

> **Coming soon.** Join the waitlist at **[orenvahealth.com](https://orenvahealth.com)**.

---

### What we're building

One health context across six consumer modules:

- **AI Doctor** — understand symptoms and know how urgently to act
- **Pharmacy** — prescriptions, refills and adherence
- **Diet & Fitness** — nutrition that reflects your actual health
- **Therapy** — mental and physical health on one record
- **Insurance** — cover and care that finally talk to each other
- **Store** — the products your care actually calls for

Information and coordination — not a replacement for your doctor.

### Where we operate

Primary market **India**, plus the **UK** and **Germany**. Built by a team in
the UK and Germany.

### Your data, in your region

Health data is sensitive, so sovereignty is a first-class commitment — not a
US-by-default afterthought:

- **Per-market residency** — India data in-region (Mumbai / `ap-south-1`); UK & EU data in London / the EEA
- **EU-hosted inference** — Mistral (Paris); no OpenAI, no US-hosted models
- **Region-pinned application** — Vercel London (`lhr1`) / Mumbai (`bom1`)
- **EU-hosted monitoring** — Sentry EU, PostHog EU

### Compliance posture

- **India** — DPDP Act 2023; ABDM-aligned
- **UK** — UK GDPR; MHRA Class I-exempt (information, not medical advice)
- **EU / Germany** — EU GDPR; BfArM / MPDG aware
- ISO 27001 — in progress · SOC 2 — readiness roadmap

Full policy pack at [Orenva-Compliance](https://github.com/Orenva-Health/Orenva-Compliance) (private).
